This app is available in four different pricing plans, including a special package for enterprises. In addition, enterprises with up to two employees can enjoy the free plan.
App is affordable and, therefore, a suitable choice for startups and small businesses on a budget. Managers use SAP HR analytics to assess employees’ needs and give the necessary support. These needs include training, compensation, and empowerment. The managers support these needs by offering appropriate learning courses and commensurating salaries with the level of service the employees provide, for example.
This app utilizes its own SuccessFactors solutions to enhance employees’ experience. These solutions support talent management and workforce planning, which helps keep employees motivated and productive.
